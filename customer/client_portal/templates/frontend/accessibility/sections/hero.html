Dycetix Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: 28/11/2025

1

Our Commitment

At Dycetix, we believe technology should empower everyone. We're committed to making our website and services accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. Accessibility is integrated into our development process from the ground up.

2

Current Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard Navigation: Full navigation via Tab, Shift+Tab, and Enter
  • Alt Text: Descriptive text for all images
  • Contrast Ratios: WCAG 2.1 compliant color contrast
  • Responsive Design: Adapts to screen sizes and 200% zoom
  • ARIA Labels: Enhanced screen reader support
3

Ongoing Improvements

We're actively auditing and refining accessibility:

  • Full screen reader compatibility (JAWS, NVDA)
  • Multimedia captions/transcripts
  • Simplified form labels and error messages

We welcome feedback to guide our efforts.

4

Request Accommodations

Need accessible formats? Contact us at solutions@dycetix.co.za within 3 business days.

5

Third-Party Tools

Enhance your experience with:

  • OS accessibility settings (text size, voice commands)
6

Report Issues

Found a barrier? Email solutions@dycetix.co.za with:

  • Page URL/service affected
  • Device/browser/assistive tools used
  • Description of the issue

We aim to respond within 5 business days.

7

Compliance Standards

We align with WCAG 2.1 Level AA using:

  • Automated testing with WAVE
  • Manual screen reader audits
  • Color contrast analyzers
8

Known Limitations

Areas we're improving:

  • Real-time form updates without screen reader alerts
  • Complex visual displays in future portfolios
9
