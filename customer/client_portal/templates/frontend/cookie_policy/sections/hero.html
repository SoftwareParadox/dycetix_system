How Dycetix Uses Cookies

Effective: 28/11/2025

Cookies help us improve your experience. This policy explains how and why we use them as we build our services.

1

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember preferences and analyze traffic.

2

How We Use Cookies

Our current approach: We use minimal, essential cookies to enhance your experience. As we introduce new services, we'll update this policy while maintaining our commitment to transparency and privacy.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functions like page navigation and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage via tools like Google Analytics
  • Future Functionality: Will support features like saved preferences if implemented
